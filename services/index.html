Services we offer

We focus our services in three areas: video game development, tool development and consulting. The sections below go into more detail on each of these areas, but they do not contain all the services that we might offer. Feel free to contact us if the service you are looking for isn’t listed below.

Game development

We specialize in the Unity game engine and have extensive experience in mobile and VR development, but we do not limit ourselves to those platforms. Within that domain, we offer:

  • Full project development. From early stages to shipping.
  • Feature development. Sometimes a project is almost there, and it just needs an extra push.
  • Bug fixing. Bugs always slip under our noses, but that doesn’t mean they should live forever.
  • Performance optimization. For when you get just a bit too ambitious for the target device’s limitations.
  • Platform porting. If your project is so good that you want to bring it to other devices.
  • Code review. An extra pair of experienced eyes might catch what went unnoticed.

Tool development

Video games are fun, but sometimes we need to get some work done. That’s why we offer the following tool development services:

  • Editor tools. We enjoy using Unity, but one tool can’t do it all. Developers often need extra tools to support their creation process, and that’s where we come in. We create tools that are crafted to improve your team’s workflow with, for example:
    • Automated mesh generation.
    • Asset management.
    • Build pipeline automation.
    • Inspector augmentation.
  • Standalone tools. Developed using Unity but used outside the engine’s editor, these tools can either aid the game development process or do something else completely. For example:
    • Sprite/image editor.
    • Home planner (architecture and/or interior design).
    • Training in Virtual Reality.

Consulting

Our background is quite diverse, spanning from Brazil, to the US, to the Netherlands and France. From mobile FPS and investigation games to Virtual Reality applications focused on healthcare and education. We can bring that experience to your project with the following services:

  • Expert input. When making impactful decisions, building reports or considering big changes.
  • Project feasibility and requirement gathering. Sometimes we know what we would like to accomplish, but not how.
  • Market insight. If you are considering entering a market and would like to get some external insight into it.
  • Advisory. For everything else that you might want some advice on, coming from an experienced developer.

Services we do not offer

Although during the years we have touched on many aspects of the multidisciplinary domain of game development, we focus on technical aspects (e.g., programming, engineering) and believe in the power that comes from specializing in it. Therefore, we do not offer the following services. Nevertheless, we can help you find the professionals required to bring your project to completion, if necessary.

  • Art. Whether it’s conceptual, 2D or 3D. We know how important art is to a game, and we know how bad we are at it. With that said, we have basic knowledge of how both render and vector graphics work, and we’re able to slightly modify them.
  • Audio design and conception. We can work with existing audio assets or help you find the perfect match for your project, but we do not create them.
  • Game design from scratch. Despite the years we’ve spent absorbing game design knowledge from the projects we’ve worked on, we expect our clients to have a good idea of what they would like to create.
  • Quality Assurance and testing. We test all the features, games and tools that we work on extensively, but we do not offer QA services for projects or features that we did not develop ourselves.
